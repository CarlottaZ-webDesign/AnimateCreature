Sully

He is a lovable monster hailing from the depths of Ringland.
His large, expressive eyes gleam with mischief and curiosity, while the blue bright colour of his body adds a touch of crazyness to his appearance.
Despite his intimidating exterior, Sully possesses a heart of gold, always willing to lend a helping hand to those in need.
In fact Sully embarks on an adventurous jurney chasing elusive doors to help the population of Ringland to catch the Aliens and lock them forever behind those doors.
